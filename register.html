CIT:

Counselors in Training (CITs) are students and former campers enrolled in grades 6-10 who are looking for a leadership position. Each CIT will receive a responsibilities form to fill out and will review the first day of camp. We love our CITs and look forward to having our former and incoming CITs join us!

Payment and Early Registration Discounts:

Full payment must be made by credit card at time of registration. We only accept Visa and MasterCard. There is an early registration discount of $50 prior to February 28th and $25 prior to May 15th. Discounted prices are reflected on website until May 15, 2020. Cash will not be accepted for camp or aftercare fees.

Refunds:

Any camper who has voluntarily withdrawn from Key School Summer Camp on or before Friday, May 15, 2021, will receive a full refund. Key School Summer Camp must be notified in writing of the withdrawal by May 15, 2021, in order for the camper to receive a refund. Refunds will not be given after the May 15, 2021 deadline. There are no refunds for absences. We will issue full refund if a student cannot attend because of a positive Covid-19 test or if the camp has been canceled.

Additional Terms:

There is no prorating of fees. We reserve the right to dismiss, without refund, any child who does not comply with the expectations of Key School Summer Camp.
