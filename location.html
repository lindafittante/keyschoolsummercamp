Camp takes place on the grounds of Francis Scott Key Elementary School, a Blue Ribbon school with a new $1.5 million outdoor renovation. Campers enjoy an artificial-turf soccer field, basketball courts, and modern playground structures in addition to a roomy indoor gymnasium. There's plenty of space to run and play!

Key School is located at 5001 Dana Place, NW, in the Palisades neighborhood of Washington DC. To drop off your happy camper, you can enter through the front door and go downstairs, or you can enter the building from the playground. Proceed to the check-in desk so the counselors can welcome your child.
